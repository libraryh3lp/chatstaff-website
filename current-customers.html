General

Thank you for being a part of the Chatstaff team! Please call, email, or chat with any questions or needs.
Our librarians value updates and feedback from your library. We offer several ways for you to share information with our staff:

  • Profile: Loaded into LibraryH3lp, the profile is a datasheet our librarians access each time they work with one of your patrons. We use an editor (linked below) to keep formatting consistent.
  • Staffing listserv: Timely news such as weather closures, databases outages, & assignment details are shared immediately with all our librarians via our listserv. Please email us for details.
  • Email: You can reach the entire admin team at info@chatstaff.org to share feedback, questions, and updates. If you staff locally, we encourage you to watch the LibraryH3lp blog for updates on new and enhanced features.
