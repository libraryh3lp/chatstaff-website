Communication is key!

Thank you for being a part of Chatstaff! Please call, email, or chat with any questions or needs.

Our librarians value updates and feedback from your library. Redesigning your website, launching a new service, or seeing a rush of questions from a class? Please let us know! We offer several ways for you to communicate with our staff:

  • Profile: Loaded into LibraryH3lp, the profile is a datasheet our librarians access each time they work with one of your patrons. We use an editor (linked below) to keep formatting consistent.
  • Staffing listserv: Timely news such as weather closures, databases outages, & assignment details are shared immediately with all our librarians via our listserv. Please email us for details.
  • Email: You can reach the entire admin team at info@chatstaff.org to share feedback, questions, and updates.

If you staff locally, we encourage you to watch the LibraryH3lp blog for updates on new and enhanced features.

Tools

  • Profile Editor : Update links, contact information, and special notes for your library's data sheet.
  • LibraryH3lp Dashboard : Review transcripts and run reports via the dashboard.
