Dedicated coverage

You never need to contribute coverage for other libraries in order to receive coverage from Chatstaff.
Have a co-op? Chatstaff can supply backup staffing to support your member libraries. Home Team First routing keeps it local when your co-op is online, and Chatstaff has your back when it is not

Fast Responses

Online patron's often won't wait for a response. Chatstaff monitors traffic patterns and adjusts staffing levels to maintain an average wait time of less than 12 seconds so that your patrons get help quickly.

Quality Service

Chatstaff’s experienced team of ALA-accredited librarians works within client libraries’ resources, focusing on the collections and guides you’ve curated for your patrons.

Flexible Options

Our mix-and-match approach lets you pick whatever schedule you need. Need help with guides, instruction, or FAQ maintenance? We can do that too!
