Welcome to Chatstaff! We look forward to working with your library and patrons.

Setting up service requires the following steps.

1. If you do not have an existing LibraryH3lp software subscription, please complete their free trial form.

2. Share some basic information about your library with us by completing our new library form.

3. Within two business days, you will receive a confirmation from us with the next steps!
