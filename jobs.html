Job Title: Chat Reference Librarian

Chatstaff, LLC

Chatstaff is a growing company offering virtual reference services to over 100 libraries across the United States. With the growth of remote library services, we're looking for an experienced, passionate and energetic librarian to join our team. Chatstaff is a professional virtual reference service staffed by librarians with MLS or equivalent degrees from ALA-accredited programs. We serve a variety of academic and public libraries across the United States and overseas. All of our librarians have chat reference experience and deeply care about the quality of service we provide, going above and beyond to help patrons. We are a women owned and librarian owned business with over 60 years combined professional expertise.

Remote part time position

Minimum of 10 hours not to exceed 25 hours per week. Some evening and weekend hours required

Job Summary

Works part time in a fast paced team environment to perform duties of chat reference for academic and public libraries. Works collaboratively with other team members to deliver quality reference interactions. Demonstrates excellent communication and listening skills along with a commitment to providing excellent service while working with a diverse population.

Responsibilities

  • Keeps up to date with information on individual libraries and updates other team members on assignments, issues, and services.
  • Keeps up with online technology including LibraryH3lp, Adobe, Jing and other applicable software.
